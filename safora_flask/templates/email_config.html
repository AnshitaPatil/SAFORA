Email Configuration

Why configure Gmail?

SMS alerts are sent via Gmail's email-to-SMS gateways. This is completely FREE and sends actual SMS messages to phone numbers.

How to get Gmail App Password:

  1. Go to Google App Passwords
  2. Enable 2-Step Verification (if not enabled)
  3. Create app password for "Mail"
  4. Copy the 16-character password
  5. Paste it below (remove spaces)
